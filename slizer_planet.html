Slizer Planet

Slizer Planet is the home of the Slizers.

Slizer Planet was divided into 7 regions, with a large dome at the intersection of all 7:

  • Fire Region, home of Torch
  • Water Region, home of Scuba
  • Ice Region, home of Ski
  • City Region, home of Turbo
  • Energy Region, home of Electro
  • Rock Region, home of Granite
  • Jungle Region, home of Amazon

Jet himself resides in the dome at the intersection of the other 7 regions.

On the eve of the new millenium, the Rock, Energy and Jungle Regions, as well as most of the dome, were destroyed by a giant meteor.
